
# System Diagnosis Playbook

The diagnosis process follows six steps.

Step 1 — Observe the outcome.

Step 2 — Identify the mechanism chain.

Step 3 — Recognize the archetype.

Step 4 — Identify structural constraints.

Step 5 — Locate leverage points.

Step 6 — Design strategic intervention.

Example:

Outcome:
Product delivery slowing.

Mechanism chain:
Growth → coordination complexity → decision bottleneck.

Archetype:
Limits to Growth.

Leverage point:
Decision authority structure.

Intervention:
Decentralize decision making.
