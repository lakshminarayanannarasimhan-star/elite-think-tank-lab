
# Archetype Master Library

System archetypes are recurring structural patterns in complex systems.

## Limits to Growth

Growth eventually slows due to emerging constraints.

Example:

Startup growth  
→ team expansion  
→ coordination complexity  
→ decision bottleneck  

## Success to the Successful

Early advantages compound into dominance.

Example:

Platform adoption → network effects → platform dominance.

## Tragedy of the Commons

Shared resources are overused by individual actors.

Example:

Multiple teams share a GPU cluster → overload.

## Escalation

Actors respond to each other's actions, creating destructive loops.

Example:

Price cuts → competitor response → price war.

## Growth and Underinvestment

Demand grows faster than infrastructure investment.

Example:

City population growth → traffic congestion.
