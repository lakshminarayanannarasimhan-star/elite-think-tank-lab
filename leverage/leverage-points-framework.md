
# Leverage Points Framework

Leverage points identify where interventions will have the greatest impact.

Ordered from weakest to strongest:

1. Parameters  
2. Buffers  
3. Physical structure  
4. Delays  
5. Balancing feedback loops  
6. Reinforcing loops  
7. Information flows  
8. Rules  
9. Self‑organization  
10. System goals  
11. Paradigms  
12. Ability to change paradigms

Strategic thinkers focus on **deep leverage points** rather than surface fixes.
