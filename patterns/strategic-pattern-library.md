Strategic patterns are high‑level dynamics observed across organizations and industries.

Examples:

Coordination Trap  
Infrastructure Lag  
Capability Trap  
Price War Spiral  
Feature War  
Platform Flywheel  
Marketplace Liquidity Loop  
Ecosystem Lock‑in  
Innovation Blindness  
Operational Fragility  
Disruption Wave  
Innovation Cycle  

Recognizing these patterns enables faster diagnosis of complex systems.
